Company Overview & Capabilities

Pentafuse Solutions is a trusted civil and structural engineering consultancy focused on high-quality design, detailing, and review services across industrial, commercial, and infrastructure sectors.

Concept Design

Concept design including basic engineering to set the project direction

Feasibility Studies

Functional, Financial viability and feasibility studies performed

Structural Analysis and Design

Structural analysis and design performed using tools like, Etabs, STAAD, etc..

Connection Design

Connection Design performed using Idea Statica and Specialized tools

Peer Reviews

Design, Fabrication and Construction feasibility, peer reviews performed for Steel, RCC and composite structures

Value Engineering

Optimized engineered solutions for Layout, Cost and Time will be advised

Earthquake Engineering

Custom Engineered Solutions using Friction and Viscous dampers

Construction Engineering Support

Construction phase assembly, erection and Site execution support

Stability Certification

Stability certificates will be provided upon review of new or existing structures

Specialized Structural Services

Fabrication Drawings

Fabrication drawings using Tekla Structures for precise steel detailing and modeling.

Preorder Engineering Support

Sales technical support for estimates

Third-Party Reviews

Third-party design reviews and approvals for quality assurance and compliance.

Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation and strengthening of existing buildings and aging structures.
